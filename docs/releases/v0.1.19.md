# Release v0.1.19

**Data artifact contract alignment: fully-withheld catalogs and optional grain
catalogs.**

## Overview

v0.1.19 tightens the fit between the portable `data-artifact/v0` structural
schema and normative prose that was already correct. Two over-constraints of the
same class are loosened: field catalogs may be fully withheld, and raw archival
grains may omit a field catalog ref. No capability-token bump and no new ADR —
both changes are pure constraint loosening inside draft `v0`.

## Highlights

### Fully-Withheld Field Catalogs

The protection model already requires that when field names are sensitive, a
producer may disclose only a withheld count. The schema previously required
`fields` to have at least one entry, forcing producers to name at least one key
to publish a catalog — a privacy inversion.

Empty `fields` is now valid when `withheld_field_count` is present and
`>= 1`. Empty `fields` without a positive count fails closed. Non-empty catalogs
are unchanged.

Prose clarifies:

- `fields: []` plus a positive count means **fully withheld under default-deny**,
  not an "empty grain";
- `withheld_field_count` is the total withheld count, including any names in
  `withheld_fields`, and MUST be `>=` the named-list length when both are
  present (validator/prose check, not schema arithmetic);
- Validation Requirements carry an explicit fully-withheld bullet.

### Optional Grain Field Catalog Ref

The standard already says field catalogs are optional for raw archival artifacts
that are not meant to be rendered or queried. The schema hard-required
`field_catalog_ref` on every grain.

That requirement is removed. Queryable or renderable grains and representations
still must have field catalogs via Validation Requirements prose. Existing
descriptors that keep the ref remain valid.

### Golden Fixtures

Two positive descriptors land under `schemas/data-artifact/v0/examples/`:

- fully-withheld catalog (`fields: []` with positive `withheld_field_count`);
- raw archival grain without `field_catalog_ref`.

Empty `fields` without a positive count fails closed under structural validation.

## Migration Notes

- **Producers with fully withheld structure:** emit `fields: []` and a positive
  `withheld_field_count` instead of inventing a dummy named field.
- **Raw archival producers:** omit `field_catalog_ref` on grains that are not
  queryable or renderable; keep catalogs on any grain or representation that is.
- **Consumers:** treat fully-withheld catalogs as default-deny; do not interpret
  empty `fields` as "no content exists." Older consumers that reject empty
  `fields` may need to re-pin to this schema revision.
- **No capability change:** the host-less token remains
  `contract: data-artifact/v0`.
